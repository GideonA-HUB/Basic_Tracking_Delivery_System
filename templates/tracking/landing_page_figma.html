About Meridian Asset Logistics

At Meridian Asset Logistics, we are revolutionizing the logistics industry by combining cutting-edge technology with strategic investment opportunities. Founded on the principle of transforming supply chain challenges into profitable ventures, we have built a comprehensive ecosystem that serves both logistics operations and investment portfolios.

Our innovative approach integrates real-time tracking, AI-powered analytics, and diversified investment platforms to deliver exceptional value to our clients. With over $2.5 billion in assets under management and operations spanning 50+ countries, we have established ourselves as a trusted leader in the global logistics and investment landscape.

Our smart trading system provides advanced analytics and AI-powered insights, helping clients make informed decisions about their logistics investments while optimizing portfolio performance in real-time.

What sets us apart is our commitment to transparency, reliability, and innovation. Our team of industry veterans brings decades of experience in managing complex supply chains and delivering consistent returns on logistics investments.
