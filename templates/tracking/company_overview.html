About Meridian Asset Logistics

Meridian Asset Logistics is a global leader in integrated logistics and investment services, providing end-to-end supply chain solutions that drive business success. Founded with a vision to revolutionize the logistics industry, we combine traditional shipping expertise with cutting-edge technology and innovative investment opportunities.

Our comprehensive platform serves businesses of all sizes, from startups to Fortune 500 companies, offering everything from maritime shipping and air freight to warehousing, distribution, and customs brokerage. We also provide exclusive investment opportunities in logistics assets, helping our clients grow their wealth while supporting the global supply chain.
